Vendor Connect - for Retailers & Suppliers

We partner with brands, hosting their catalog on our cloud servers so that retailers can get access to the latest products and images. Select which items you want, or all of them, and our solution will import those products directly into your POS/Inventory system. No data entry needed. Time saved. With this access, you can send purchase orders to your brands for store replenishment or drop shipping- in-store or online.

Every customer wants to find what they’re looking for every time they visit a store or go to website... and you make sure that happens with Vendor Connect.

With Vendor Connect, retailers and suppliers are collaborating to exchange real-time information like catalogs, inventory, and orders fast, saving time and increasing sales. Connect POS and eCommerce systems to suppliers electronically, eliminating manual data entry. Sell in-store, out-of-stock and catalog items with an Endless Aisle, then delight customers by drop-shipping right to their front door, just like online retailers.

ELECTRONIC CATALOGS - NO MORE MANUAL ENTRY

Vendor Connect allows suppliers and retailers to publish and receive product catalogs electronically, eliminating the need to enter item data by hand, recovering hundreds or even thousands of wasted man-hours. Point and click to select items to carry in-store, on the website, or drop-ship right from the supplier. Put the right items on the shelf faster to sell more and keep inventory to a minimum.

ELECTRONIC ORDERS - LEVEL THE PLAYING FIELD

Vendor Connect will reduce time spent on creating and submitting purchase orders with paperwork and messy excel spreadsheets by up to 80 – 90%. Placing orders is now fast and easy and can be sent right from POS or ecommerce systems to any supplier for drop-shipping. Even the smallest retailers can now go electronic with their suppliers, saving big for everyone.

ENDLESS AISLE - NEVER MISS A SALE

It isn’t possible for retailers to carry every product in inventory that their suppliers offer, but they can still sell them with the Endless Aisle. Vendor Connect makes it possible for retailers to stock the ideal products for their local market, and never miss out on a sale for an item they don’t carry or that might be out of stock. Suppliers can now sell their entire catalog through every channel to increase brand loyalty and sales.
