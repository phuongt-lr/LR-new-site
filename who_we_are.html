Why You'll Want To Partner With Us

We understand retail. We've worked it, managed it, and our roots are retail technology. We started in 2010 with eCommerce Connect. Our goal was to build an integration that would make our clients lives easier by managing their online webstores, all from right within Retail Pro. In 2013, Linked Retail introduced another powerful tool for their clients- Vendor Connect. Now with the direct link to their brands, store replenishment is simplified, and customers always get what they want through drop shipping.
